# Computer Forensics and Cybercrime (7CCSMCFC) Revision Guide

**Credits:** 15 | **Semester:** Spring | **Assessment:** Likely Examination + Coursework

## Module Overview
This module applies forensic techniques to investigate cyber crimes. They will cover:
- Legal aspects of cybersecurity (UK Computer Misuse Act, GDPR, international legislation)
- Forensic techniques and methodologies
- Incident management and digital evidence handling
- The general cybercrime landscape with real-world scenario application

## Preparation & Prerequisites
- **Prerequisites:** They must complete the Cryptography module first.
- **Background Knowledge:** Basic legal awareness and cryptography fundamentals are helpful.

## Reading List
- **Primary Text:** Carrier, *File System Forensic Analysis* (2005) (Approx. £40-55).

They should compile revision materials concerning digital evidence handling, forensic methodology overviews, and summaries of key IT legislation (CMA, GDPR) in this folder.
