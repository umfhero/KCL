# Security Management (7CCSMSEM) Revision Guide

**Credits:** 15 | **Semester:** Autumn | **Assessment:** Likely mix of Examination and Coursework (Essays/Reports)

## Module Overview
This is the least technical compulsory module, providing a management and policy lens. They will study:
- Risk assessment and management
- Security auditing and business continuity planning
- Cost-effective cybersecurity strategy and risk communication
- Regulatory frameworks and security governance

## Preparation & Prerequisites
- **Background Knowledge:** Prior knowledge of organisational IT structures is helpful but not required. Students from purely technical backgrounds sometimes find the essay-based assessment unfamiliar, so practicing academic writing could be beneficial.

## Reading List
- **Primary Text:** Whitman & Mattord, *Management of Information Security*, 6th Ed. (2018) (Approx. £50-80).
- **Free Alternatives:** They should read the NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0, SP 800-53 (Security Controls), and SP 800-37 (Risk Management Framework), freely available from nist.gov.

They can use this space to compile notes on regulatory frameworks, risk communication, essay drafts, and NIST framework summaries.
